KARSA JOB - COMPANY ROLE DESIGN PROMPT

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
Professional, trustworthy, data-driven interface for company users. Conveys authority and innovation while maintaining consistency with student role. Talent discovery based on verified project data, not self-written CVs.

Principles: Evidence-based trust with verification badges | Professional excellence with refined typography | Impact-driven discovery prioritizing metrics | Seamless consistency with student design

VISUAL SYSTEM

Typography: Space Grotesk (300-700) headings, Inter (300-900) body | Colors: #000000 headings, #374151 body, #6B7280 secondary | Letter-spacing: -0.02em headings

Color Palette:
• Primary Blue: #004D99, #0066CC (trust, professionalism)
• Success Green: #4D9900, #66CC00 (verified, impact)
• Executive Indigo: #4F46E5, #6366F1 (CTAs, metrics)
• Status: #3B82F6 received, #FBBF24 shortlisted, #A855F7 interview, #10B981 hired, #EF4444 rejected

Backgrounds:
• Hero: linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(0,77,153,0.40) 0%, rgba(79,70,229,0.35) 50%, rgba(107,114,128,0.30) 100%), parallax fixed, 480px min-height
• Mesh: radial-gradient blue/indigo/green corners (0.06 opacity)
• Glassmorphism: rgba(255,255,255,0.25), backdrop-blur 20px
